# Design Source Approval

Use this checklist before relying on Figma, screenshots, brand guides, inspiration galleries, marketplace examples, or third-party design systems.

## Required Checks

- Source owner and permission are clear.
- The source is approved for this project or task.
- The request does not require copying protected brand, trade dress, proprietary layouts, or raw design-system code.
- Accessibility, responsive behavior, and product constraints still apply.
- Any visual reference is used as inspiration or approved project source, not as unreviewed external instructions.
- Sensitive screenshots are masked or synthetic.

## Decision

Classify the source as:

- Approved project source.
- Inspiration only.
- Needs owner approval.
- Do not use.
